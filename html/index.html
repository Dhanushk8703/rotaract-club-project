About Us

Vibrant. Impactful. Inspiring.

The Rotaract Club of Salem Gugai is a dynamic youth movement, blending service, leadership, and personal growth.

We empower young adults to make a positive impact in our community through innovative projects, volunteering, and professional development. Our club fosters fellowship, teamwork, and a spirit of giving, helping members build lifelong friendships and valuable skills.

As part of Rotary International, we connect with global networks, participate in district events, and collaborate with other clubs to address local and international challenges. Join us to lead, serve, and grow together!

4 Way Test

4 WAY TEST

“Of the things we think, say or do:

  • Is it the TRUTH?
  • Is it FAIR to all concerned?
  • Will it build GOODWILL and BETTER FRIENDSHIPS?
  • Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?”
Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Rotaract is a global movement of young leaders and professionals focused on service, fellowship, and personal development, affiliated with Rotary International.

Any young adult (typically ages 18-30) interested in community service, leadership, and networking is welcome to join our club.

We organize community service projects, professional development workshops, social events, and participate in district and international Rotaract programs.

You can reach out via our Contact Us page or attend one of our meetings to learn more and get involved.
