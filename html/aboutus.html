Our Rotaract Story

Rotaract Club of Salem Gugai is a dynamic youth organization affiliated with Rotary International. We are a group of young professionals and students aged 18-30 who are passionate about community service, professional development, and international understanding.

Founded in 2010, our club has grown to become one of the most active Rotaract clubs in the region, with over 50 members from diverse backgrounds working together to make a difference in our community.

Key Achievements

  • Organized annual blood donation camps collecting 500+ units
  • Conducted career guidance programs for 2000+ students
  • Adopted 5 government schools for infrastructure development
  • Recognized as "Best Rotaract Club" at district level 3 times
